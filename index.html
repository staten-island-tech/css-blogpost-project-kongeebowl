Flamecharm is one of the best if not THE best attunement for PvE in Deepwoken. It has high damage, insane sustain, and most importantly, BROKEN talents. Flamecharm also has great mantras for utility such as Graceful Flame, which can be used in things like Diluvian and Primadon, making a temporary campfire wherever, whenever. Click to see more.


Frostdraw is a tough second to Flamecharm when takling about PvE. Unlike Flamecharm, Frostdraw's strength lies in its mantras. It can deal heavy damage, allowing you to easily get the healthpacks from the enemies, making there no need for any built in sustain inside the attunements. Especially in Diluvian, mantras such as Warden's Blades are perfect for high AoE damage. Click to see more.


Galebreathe is absolutely BROKEN if you know how to use it. Although it might not seem like much, its mantras have great utility, damage, posture damage, and much, much more. It's easy to pick up and its mantras have such good synergy you can just spam everything to kill most enemies, making galebreathe very easy to pick up as well. Click to see more.
